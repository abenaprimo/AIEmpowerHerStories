About the project

Public narratives still too often suggest that women—especially caregivers—are less suited for leadership, innovation, or emerging fields like AI. When these stories are missing, the future of work becomes narrower than real life.

AI EmpowerHer Stories changes that. We capture and share compelling, true stories of women using AI to build content businesses, apps, and digital services—while navigating the realities of caregiving, family responsibilities, and community life.

Our north star

Make it normal to see women—especially caregivers and women of color—as leaders, innovators, and builders in the AI economy.

When women thrive at work, the benefits ripple across families, communities, and the broader economy.

How it works

1) Story Studio

We document real journeys through interviews, field recording, and documentary-style production.

2) Digital Library

A searchable, public collection of “Women Building AI” stories with accessible formats and transcripts.

3) Distribution

We publish multi-channel campaigns (short video, audio, essays) tailored to different audiences and contexts.

4) National Showcase

An annual virtual event that spotlights transformative journeys and invites public conversation.

5) Evaluation

We track reach, engagement, and narrative sentiment to learn what shifts perceptions over time.

Expected impact

Shift biased attitudes

Repeated exposure to credible, counter-stereotypical stories broadens what people believe is possible.

Expand narratives of success

We highlight caregiving-inclusive pathways so “leadership” reflects real lives—not outdated archetypes.

Increase public awareness

We elevate innovations and workplace pathways that deserve attention and can scale across society.

How we’ll measure progress

  • Audience reach and engagement across platforms
  • Sentiment and narrative analysis of story uptake
  • Participant feedback and longitudinal follow-up
  • Media pickup and partner amplification (as developed)
